About Us

Moonlight group consists of Moonlight Cake House Sdn. Bhd. (MCHSB) involved in producing, marketing and retailing varieties of cakes and pastries; Moonlight Cafe Sdn. Bhd. (MCSB) involved in provision of local and western food and beverage for dine in customers; and Moonlight Manufacturing Sdn. Bhd. (MMSB) involved in sourcing of materials, manufacturing, processing and packing of all kinds of materials supplied to the above 2 companies.......
  In year 2007, Moonlight started their business as MCHSB located at Taman Gaya principally involved in marketing and retailing varieties of cakes and pastries, subsequently in year 2010, the MCSB was set up to provide cakes, pastries, local and western food and beverage for dine in customers. As the turnover continue to grow tremendously, in year 2012 the MMSB was set up with own factory located at Tampoi, Johor Bahru to manufacturing and marketing varieties of cakes and pastries exclusively for the MCHSB and MCSB.
  Today, the group has its owned duties at several prominent and strategic location namely Taman Sutera Utama (headoffice) and the shoplot is owned by the group), Johor, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur and Selangor area.
  What is your favourite cake? No matter what your taste bids crave, chances are we have a variety of delicious cakes and pastries for you. Beside cakes & pastries, we also serve local food and western food. We offer varieties of food & beverage for your selection, from the quick bite to a slow coffee dessert, all these appetizing treats is here for you to savor.
  We are committed to continuing our positive relationship with local business owners and residents, and encourage our customer to let us know how we are doing. To ask a question, or provide us with feedback, please fill out our feedback form.

Thank you for visiting our Website, we hope to meet you soon!
